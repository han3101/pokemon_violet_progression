Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza through the West Gate and angle southwest toward South Province's western road network.
  2. Cut through the southwestern approach and enter Alfornada Cavern rather than trying to force mountain walls.
  3. Use Gliding on the final elevated section to shorten the cavern-side approach.
  4. Exit into Alfornada and head into town for Tulip's Gym Test and battle.

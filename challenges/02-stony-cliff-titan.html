Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza through the West Gate and pass through Cortondo.
  2. Keep following the path northwest into South Province (Area Three).
  3. Take the walkable cliff path into the stony basin where Klawf appears.
  4. After the first phase, continue forward to the herb cave for the second phase with Arven.

Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza via the West Gate and ride through Cortondo toward West Province (Area One).
  2. Keep northwestern to the Segin Squad approach road; Swimming is unlocked but not required here.
  3. Take the final branch into Team Star territory.
  4. Enter the base, clear the Star Barrage, and Giacomo appears at the boss arena.

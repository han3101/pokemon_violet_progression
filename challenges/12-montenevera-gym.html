Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza through the East Gate and follow the road toward Zapapico.
  2. Continue north onto the established mountain route into Glaseado Mountain.
  3. Use High Jump for small route optimizations on the switchbacks, then keep following the road into Montenevera.
  4. Ryme's Gym is in town after the rap-battle style Gym Test.

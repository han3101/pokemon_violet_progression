Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza via the East Gate and ride toward Artazon.
  2. Continue northeast into Tagtree Thicket on the wooded road network.
  3. Use High Jump where helpful to hop minor ledges and keep a direct line through the thicket.
  4. Follow the final path into the Navi Squad Base and clear the Star Barrage before Atticus.

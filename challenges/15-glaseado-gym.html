Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza through the East Gate and follow the main road toward Zapapico.
  2. Continue onto the northern mountain route and climb the standard switchbacks into Glaseado Mountain.
  3. Use Gliding on the broad upper slopes to trim the final approach to the Snow Slope Run area.
  4. The Gym battle with Grusha happens at the mountaintop after the slope-running Gym Test.

  • Grusha Terastallizes Altaria into an Ice type.

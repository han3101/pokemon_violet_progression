Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza through the West Gate and follow the main road into South Province (Area Two).
  2. Stay on the westbound road as it curves downhill toward Cortondo.
  3. Enter Cortondo and continue to the center of town.
  4. The Gym is in Cortondo proper once you complete the Gym Test.

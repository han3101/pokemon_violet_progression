Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza through the West Gate and head northwest through Medali toward Casseroya Lake.
  2. Reach the lakeshore from the southern side of the lake network.
  3. Use Swimming to cross to the Titan area; Gliding helps chain together the final islets cleanly.
  4. Follow the scripted encounters between Dondozo and Tatsugiri until the final battle phase with Arven.

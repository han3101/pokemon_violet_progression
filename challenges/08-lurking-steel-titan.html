Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza through the East Gate and ride to Artazon.
  2. Continue northeast and then inland along the East Province (Area Three) mining route.
  3. Follow the canyon road toward Orthworm's burrow zone.
  4. After the first phase, follow Orthworm to the herb cave for the second battle with Arven.

Recommended path

  1. Exit Mesagoza through the West Gate and head through Cortondo.
  2. Continue northwest into West Province (Area One) using Dash to stay on the long ridge road.
  3. Follow the cliffside path up to the Bombirdier nesting area.
  4. Proceed into the Titan zone and follow the scripted retreat for the second phase.

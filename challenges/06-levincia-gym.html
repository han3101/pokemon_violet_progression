Recommended path

  1. Leave Mesagoza through the East Gate and follow the road to Artazon.
  2. Stay on the eastbound coastal route from Artazon into the Levincia approach.
  3. Continue onto the bridge and city road that lead directly into Levincia.
  4. The Gym is in the main city district after Iono's streamer challenge.

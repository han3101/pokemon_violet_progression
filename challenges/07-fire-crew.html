Recommended path

  1. Use the East Gate and ride toward Artazon.
  2. Turn north through East Province (Area One) and follow the highland road to the Schedar Squad side.
  3. Stay on the main pass; Swimming is available but does not meaningfully improve this route.
  4. Enter the base and clear the Star Barrage before facing Mela.

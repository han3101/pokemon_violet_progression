Recommended path

  1. Ride out through the West Gate and head back toward West Province by way of South Province (Area Two).
  2. Cut through the southern edge of Asado Desert toward Cascarrafa.
  3. Use High Jump to shorten low ridge crossings and stay on the cleanest line through the sand.
  4. Enter Cascarrafa from the east side and continue to the Gym area after the market errand.
